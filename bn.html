About Movie:-

Bheemla Nayak is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Saagar K Chandra from a screenplay by Trivikram Srinivas. It is a remake of the 2020 Malayalam film Ayyappanum Koshiyum. The film stars Pawan Kalyan, Rana Daggubati, Nithya Menen and Samyuktha Menon.

Box office:-

Bheemla Nayak grossed ₹161 – 193 crore worldwide in its theatrical run.On its opening day, the film grossed ₹52.1 crore worldwide, including ₹43.1 crore in the domestic territory and US$1 million from overseas.The netted ₹37 crore in India and emerged as the best post-pandemic opener in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Box Office India reported that the lower ticket prices in Andhra coupled with stricter norms by the state government has negatively impacted the film's collections.In its opening weekend, the film grossed ₹110 crore worldwide, of which ₹89.40 crore came from India, and the rest from the overseas.The film grossed ₹50 crore in 6 days, with the distributor's share standing at ₹55 crore. After 10 days of its theatrical run, the film collected a worldwide distributor's share of ₹100 crore, with a gross of ₹167 crore.The film became a profitable venture in the United States, grossing $2.4 million in 11 days.
